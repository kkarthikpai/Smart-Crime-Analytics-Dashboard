Welcome to Smart Crime Analytics

This dashboard provides data-driven visualization and analysis of crime statistics across 19 major Indian metropolitan cities. Leveraging data from the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), the platform offers crucial insights into crime trends, city hot spots, and offense distribution over time. Use the **Analytics** dropdown above to explore detailed charts and findings.
